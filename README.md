## 🎯 Course Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

- 🧮 **Understand and apply** fundamental mathematical concepts (Discrete Math, Graph Theory, Probability, Linear Algebra) in AI
- 📈 **Develop proficiency** in advanced techniques (Bayesian Theory, Optimization, PCA) to solve complex AI problems
- 🤖 **Analyze and design** AI models using mathematical frameworks (Search Algorithms, Neural Networks, LDA)
- 🔬 **Critically assess and implement** mathematical models in real-world AI applications

## 📖 Weekly Schedule

| Week | Instructor | Lecture Title | Topics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Dr. Ahmad/Jawed | **Introduction to the Course** | Overview of AI, Importance of Mathematical Models, Course Structure |
| 2-3 | Dr. Ahmad | **Discrete Math & Graph Theory** | Vectors, Norms, Distances, Linear Combinations, Orthogonalization |
| 4-5 | Dr. Jawed | **Trees & Search Algorithms** | Binary Trees, Minimax, Alpha-Beta Pruning, Game Theory |
| 6-8 | Dr. Ahmad | **Probability & Multivariable Calculus** | Random Variables, Distributions, Gradients, Hessians |
| 9-10 | Dr. Jawed | **Bayesian Theory & Probabilistic Models** | Bayesian Inference, Naive Bayes, HMMs |
| 11-13 | Dr. Ahmad | **Linear Algebra & Optimization** | Eigenvalues, SVD, Gradient Descent, Convex Optimization |
| 14-16 | Dr. Jawed | **Dimensionality Reduction & Neural Networks** | PCA, t-SNE, LDA, Perceptrons, Backpropagation |

## 📚 Course Materials

### 🔥 Core Textbooks

1. **"Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach"** by Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig (4th Edition, 2020)
2. **"Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning"** by Christopher M. Bishop
3. **"Pattern Classification"** by Duda, Hart, & Stork (2nd Edition)
4. **"Linear Algebra and Its Applications"** by Gilbert Strang

### 📖 Reference Materials

1. **"Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications"** by Kenneth H. Rosen
2. **"Machine Learning: A Probabilistic Perspective"** by Kevin P. Murphy
3. [Stanford Online: Andrew Ng's Machine Learning (Coursera)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/machine-learning) 🌐
